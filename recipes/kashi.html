It's a traditional Coffee in Kashan, city in Tehran, Iran.

ingredients

  • Fresh grinded Coffee
  • Cocoa
  • Rose water
  • Honey or Sugar

steps

  1. pour water and Coffee in a large pot onto a boil
  2. let it boil for about 3 hours
  3. add the Cocoa pouder and gently mix them
  4. pour Rose water and mix again
  5. add some honey to your cups
  6. enjoy
